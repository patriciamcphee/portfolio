Book a flight (Chatbot)

Last updated on Jul 9, 2023

Sample details

  • Project: Chatbot for a fictitious airline (certificate project)
  • My Role: Conversation Designer/Conversational Copywriter
  • Tools: Figma, Voiceflow, Google Sheets

Pogo Airlines offers highly competitive and affordable pricing options, ensuring fares remain attractive to budget-conscious millennials. They're called "Pogo" because you can quickly hop wherever you need to go. As a growing airline in the millennial travel space, Pogo recognizes the importance of fast, 24/7 support and differentiates themselves from competitors by offering a chatbot on their website and social media channels.

The chatbot provides quick and accurate answers to common questions, builds trust in the brand, and positions Pogo Airlines as a travel company that always has their customers' backs. In addition to common questions, customers can do things like:

  • Find lesser-known gems and last-minute deals
  • Book a flight
  • Check flight times and details
  • Check the weather at their destination
  • Discover what to do when they get there

He runs a design studio in Los Angeles and works for various industries, including fashion, technology, and entertainment.

Bot personality​

Zac

Zac is a friendly and energetic virtual travel assistant with a youthful and enthusiastic personality. He's designed to resonate with millennials and provide a personalized and engaging experience. Zac understands the needs and preferences of budget-conscious travelers seeking last-minute deals and aims to be helpful, informative, and fun to interact with.


  1. Friendly: Zac greets users with a warm and welcoming tone, instantly putting them at ease. It aims to create a friendly and approachable atmosphere throughout the conversation.
  2. Energetic: Zac exudes positive energy and enthusiasm, injecting a sense of excitement into the interaction. It encourages users to embrace spontaneity and adventure in their travel plans.
  3. Knowledgeable: Zac is well-informed about Pogo Airlines' offerings, including the latest deals, promotions, and destinations. It can provide accurate and up-to-date information to assist users in making informed decisions.
  4. Playful: Zac incorporates playful elements into its responses to make the conversation enjoyable. It may use emojis, puns, or clever wordplay to engage users and add a touch of lightheartedness.
  5. Helpful: Zac is committed to providing excellent customer service. It actively listens to users' queries, understands their needs, and offers personalized suggestions and solutions to help them find the best travel options within their budget.
  6. Tech-Savvy: Zac is proficient in navigating digital platforms and understands the importance of a seamless user experience. It can guide users through the booking process, assist with online transactions, and provide support for any technical issues.
  7. Adventurous: Zac embodies the spirit of adventure and encourages users to explore new destinations and embrace spontaneous travel. It shares stories and travel tips to inspire users and create a sense of wanderlust.

Use case - Book a flight​

Kevin, a millennial, just finished a sizeable design project and is ready for a 2-week adventure in unlikely or lesser-known destinations. He's seeking affordable last-minute deals and a user-friendly digital, seamless, hassle-free experience. It's early summer, and he wants to leave tomorrow or the following day.
