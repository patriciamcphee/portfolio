FitFuel+ is a lifestyle brand that aims to be a comprehensive fitness partner providing personalized support, guidance, nutrition advice, and plant-based protein powder to elevate users' performance. 

FitFuel+ aims to address the challenge of individuals seeking effective fitness support and guidance to achieve their fitness goals:

- Many individuals struggle to create personalized workout routines that align with their fitness goals, abilities, and preferences.
- Finding high-quality protein powder products that complement their workout routines can be challenging, leading to confusion and potentially choosing products that don't meet their needs.

To address these challenges, FitFuel+ can empower individuals to take charge of their fitness aspirations with a chatbot. The bot's purpose is to provide personalized workout routines to each user's fitness level, preferences, and goals. It can create dynamic and adaptive workout plans that can evolve with the user's progress. Additionally, it aims to promote the sale of protein powder as a supplement to support their fitness journey.

## Use case - Check lab results

Megan, a Marketing Executive, decided to check her lab results while she was at her office during her lunch break. She had been feeling a bit off lately and had some medical tests done, and she couldn't wait any longer to know the results.

Sitting alone at her desk, she opened her laptop and logged into the patient portal. Her office space was quiet as most of her colleagues were on lunch breaks. The soft hum of the air conditioning and the distant sound of colleagues chatting in the break room provided a backdrop to her actions.

Megan's feelings were a mix of anticipation and mild anxiety. She had been distracted all morning, and the looming uncertainty about her health was weighing on her mind. Her desk was cluttered with papers, and a somewhat tense mood had replaced her usually cheerful demeanor.

As she navigated through the patient portal using the chatbot, her fingers tapped the keyboard with a nervous energy. She hoped for good news but was mentally prepared to face any outcomes. The sterile office environment and the clock ticking on her desk seemed to emphasize the seriousness of the situation, amplifying her sense of anticipation.

        <li>Boost energy levels to remain active and independent in her senior years.</li>

        <li>Importance of independence; doesn't want to rely on others for basic activities.</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
  </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><b>Demands/preferences</b></td>
      <td>
        <ul>
          <li>Preferably, a program with low-impact, easy-to-follow exercises like walking routines, chair exercises, and gentle yoga.</li>
          <li>Would appreciate a protein powder mix that is plant-based, easy to digest, and specifically formulated for seniors.</li>
          <li>Prefers communication methods that she is comfortable with, like email and phone support, instead of newer tech platforms.</li>
        </ul>
      </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td><b>Information needs</b></td>
      <td>
        <ul>
          <li>Information on low-impact exercises that can help her achieve her fitness goals.</li>
          <li>Guidance on the type and amount of protein powder suitable for seniors.</li>
          <li>Diet tips that focus on her specific nutritional needs as a senior citizen.</li>
          <li>Simple and straightforward instructions for any technical products or services.</li>
        </ul>
      </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td><b>Anxieties and concerns</b></td>
      <td>
        <ul>
          <li>Wary of injury or strain; wants a fitness routine tailored to seniors.</li>
          <li>Skeptical about the need for protein powder at her age and how it might affect her health.</li>
          <li>Fear of adopting a routine that might exacerbate age-related health conditions.</li>
          <li>Concerns about the complexity of using tech-based solutions and the risk of feeling overwhelmed.</li>
          <li>Uncertainty about the safety and benefits of using protein powder at her age.</li>
        </ul>
      </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td><b>Expectations</b></td>
      <td>
        <ul>
          <li>Personalized workout routines that are tailored to her fitness level and limitations.</li>
          <li>Nutritional guidance backed by scientific research and specifically designed for seniors.</li>
          <li>Easily accessible customer support that can guide her through the technical aspects if needed.</li>
        </ul>
      </td>
  </tr>
    <tr>
    <td><b>Technical proficiency</b></td>
      <td>
        <ul>
          <li>Limited technical skills; can operate basic apps on her smartphone but is not overly comfortable with technology.</li>
          <li>Would prefer simplified, straightforward user interfaces and step-by-step guidance for any tech-related fitness solutions.</li>
          <li>Limited experience with chatbots or voice assistance. Finds them confusing and would prefer human interaction for more personalized and empathetic support.</li>
        </ul>
      </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td><b>Other assumptions</b></td>
      <td>
        <ul>
          <li>Likely has more free time to commit to a fitness routine but less willingness to spend that time learning complex systems or exercises.</li>
          <li>May has some basic nutrition understanding but needs guidance on newer concepts like protein supplementation.</li>
        </ul>
      </td>
  </tr>
</table>

### Bot needs

<table>
  <tr>
  <td><b>Fulfillment capability</b></td>
  <td>
    <ul>
      <li>Provides knowledge on exercises and routines catered to individual fitness levels.</li>
      <li>Offers protein shake recommendations for post-workout recovery.</li>
      <li>Emphasizes mental and emotional well-being.</li>
      <li>Delivers advice and suggestions on self-care and celebrates user milestones.</li>
    </ul>
  </td>
  </tr>
    <tr>
  <td><b>Can provide this info</b></td>
  <td>
    <ul>
      <li>Benefits of protein shakes for different ages and fitness levels.</li>
      <li>Suitable low-impact exercises for seniors.</li>
      <li>Emotional support, self-care tips, and motivational encouragement.</li>
      <li>Knowledge on health, human performance, and applied exercise physiology.</li>
    </ul>
  </td>
  </tr>
    <tr>
  <td><b>Limitations</b></td>
  <td>
    <ul>
      <li>Might not have specific medical advice or recommendations for certain conditions (unless programmed).</li>
      <li>Doesn't replace human therapists or personal trainers in certain complex fitness scenarios.</li>
    </ul>
  </td>
  </tr>
    <tr>
  <td><b>Must ask this info</b></td>
  <td>
    <ul>
      <li>Any existing health conditions or injuries.</li>
      <li>Frequency of workouts preferred per week.</li>
      <li>User's comfort level with different exercises.</li>
      <li>Dietary restrictions if any, before recommending protein powders.</li>
    </ul>
  </td>
  </tr>
    <tr>
  <td><b>Has this info already</b></td>
  <td>
    <ul>
      <li>Benefits of protein shakes for post-workout recovery.</li>
      <li>A wide range of exercises suitable for different age groups, including seniors.</li>
      <li>Information related to emotional well-being and mental health in the context of fitness.</li>
    </ul>
  </td>
  </tr>
    <tr>
  <td><b>Must provide this info</b></td>
  <td>
    <ul>
      <li>Personalized workout routine based on Sarah's preferences and needs.</li>
      <li>Recommendations on how and when to use protein powders, especially for seniors.</li>
      <li>Motivational quotes or encouragement after each workout session.</li>
      <li>Celebratory messages upon achieving milestones.</li>
    </ul>
  </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
  <td><b>User behavior</b></td>
  <td>
    <ul>
      <li>Sarah will likely approach Ryan with questions about low-impact exercises suitable for her age and fitness level.</li>
      <li>She may inquire about the benefits of protein powder for seniors and how to incorporate it into her diet.</li>
      <li>Sarah will value the emotional support and empathetic responses from Ryan, especially considering her beginner status.</li>
      <li>She might need repetitive guidance, step-by-step instructions, and consistent motivation.</li>
    </ul>
  </td>
  </tr>
</table>
