Frustration or pain points​

Frustration or pain point

System Admin

Network Engineer

IT Architect

Homegrown scripts for deploying legacy apps that have mysterious voodoo power. Difficult to reproduce application deployments consistently. 
The proliferation of various management console interfaces.  
Today, network engineers think they need to be developers.  
Human error may occur as a result of repetitive manual tasks.  
Troubleshooting issues can be hard and confusing. 
Not able to monitor sessions in real time. 
Provisioning IT infrastructure, such as servers, can take weeks, sometimes longer. 
Non-functional requirements are difficult to plan. I don’t always know in advance my traffic expectations or clearly understand my clustering or capacity needs.   
Lack of proper and thorough implementation/integration documentation.
