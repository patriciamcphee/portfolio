<h2>About Me!</h2>

My journey into Technical Writing started over 20 years ago on a Human-Centered Design & Engineering team at a large telecommunications company. There I learned how to conduct usability tests (participant observation and user behavior) to uncover and solve user experience challenges with end-user documentation.

I'm a word nerd who loves to explain complex concepts in simple language. I can spot ambiguities and logical inconsistencies from a mile away, and I get a kick out of style guides and know when to follow the rules and when to break them.
