Welcome to MedConnect Medical Center. Thank you for calling. Your health is our priority and we are dedicated to providing you with quality healthcare services. Please listen carefully as our menu options have recently changed.

- If this is an emergency, hang up and dial 911 or visit your nearest emergency room.
- If you wish to schedule, reschedule, or cancel an appointment, press 1.
- If you require prescription refills, test results, or have questions related to medication, press 2.
- For billing and insurance-related inquiries, including payment options and claims, press 3.
- If you need to speak with a nurse or have a medical question, please press 4.
- If you are a healthcare professional seeking to collaborate or communicate with our office, press 5.
- For any other inquiries or to speak with a representative, press 0.

For your convenience, you can also visit our website at [www.mcmedicalcenter.com](http://www.mcmedicalcenter.com/) for online appointment scheduling, patient forms, and more.

Thank you for calling MedConnect Medical Center. We appreciate your patience and look forward to assisting you with your healthcare needs.
